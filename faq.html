Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about dimensional investments

What is MultiversMarket?

+

MultiversMarket is the premier platform for dimensional investments, allowing users to acquire, develop, and profit from unique interdimensional spaces. We offer a curated selection of dimensions with various properties, environments, and potential applications.

How do I purchase a dimension?

+

Purchasing a dimension is simple: browse our marketplace, select a dimension that interests you, add it to your cart, and complete the checkout process. Once your purchase is confirmed, you'll receive access credentials to your new dimension within 24 hours.

Are dimensions safe to access?

+

All dimensions available on MultiversMarket undergo rigorous stability testing and safety certification. Each dimension includes a detailed safety rating and recommended precautions. However, as with any interdimensional travel, we recommend following our safety guidelines and starting with supervised exploration.

What can I do with my dimension?

+

The possibilities are virtually limitless! Customers use their dimensions for research, resource extraction, tourism development, creative projects, private retreats, and even establishing new civilizations. Each dimension includes suggested use cases based on its unique properties.

How do I access my dimension after purchase?

+

After completing your purchase, you'll receive a dimensional access key and coordinates. You can access your dimension through our proprietary portal technology, available at any of our access centers worldwide, or through our home portal device (sold separately).

What is your refund policy?

+

Due to the unique nature of dimensional acquisitions, all sales are final. However, we offer a 30-day stability guarantee. If your dimension experiences unexpected instability within 30 days of purchase, we'll provide a replacement dimension of equal or greater value.

Can I modify my dimension?

+

Yes, most dimensions can be modified to some extent. The degree of customization depends on the dimension's fundamental properties. Our Dimension Modification Guide provides detailed information on safe and effective dimensional engineering techniques.

Do you offer dimensional maintenance services?

+

Yes, we offer various maintenance packages to ensure your dimension remains stable and functions optimally. Our team of dimensional engineers can perform regular stability checks, repair dimensional anomalies, and implement requested modifications.
